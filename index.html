Our Story

Established in 1982 by a self-made business visionary, Mr. Mohammed Bin Abdullah Al Othman, in Hofuf, Eastern Province, Saudi Arabia, NADA has always been a family-owned company. Over the years, it has become a leading producer, manufacturer, and distributor of dairy, juices, and food products in Saudi Arabia, GCC, and Iraq.

Throughout Nada’s 40+ years of history, we have established a proven track record of product innovations and market-firsts for our consumers.

NADA today is part of a reputable & diversified business group, Al Othman Holding, which includes companies in plastic manufacturing, gas and oil, contracting, heavy equipment trading, hotels and resorts, IT consulting, travel, education, insurance, and healthcare.
